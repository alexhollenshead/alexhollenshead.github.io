The Cowboy Classes

The 3 Cowboy Classes all want to take over the world, and each for different reasons.

Soon, all different types of Cowboys will meet each other in an old fashioned showdown.

ALIENS

THE ALIEN COWBOYS CAME FROM THE PLANET SEPTRON AND INVADED PLANET EARTH DURING THE WESTERN GOLD RUSH. SINCE THEY HAVE THE ABILITY TO SHAPESHIFT, THEY EASILY MIXED IN WITH HUMANS AND SEEKED THE MUCH WANTED GOLD. AFTER DISCOVERING THE POWER THAT COMES WITH WEALTH, THEY DEDICATED THEMSELVES TO BEING THE ELITE ONE PERCENT OF THE WORLD AND TO CONQUER THROUGH WEALTH AND STEALTH.

CYBORGS

THE CYBORGS, ONCE HUMAN, NOW TURNED HALF ROBOT, WERE CREATED BY SCIENTIST EXPERIMENTS THAT TRIED TO EXPAND THE INTELLECTUAL CAPABILITIES OF THE HUMAN. THROUGH THE EXPERIMENTS, THE CYBORGS GAINED INFINITE KNOWLEDGE AND NOW SEEK TO TAKE OVER WORLD TO MAKE IT PERFECT.

ZOMBIES

THE ZOMBIE COWBOYS WERE ON EARTH FROM THE VERY BEGINNING. THEY HAVE JUST STARTED TO COME OUT OF THEIR HIDING UNDERGROUND AND ARE NOW SEEKING REVENGE FOR THEIR TIME SPENT UNDER THE EARTH. THE ZOMBIES COME IN HORDES AND SEEK TO TAKE OVER THROUGH THEIR STRENGTH IN NUMBERS.
